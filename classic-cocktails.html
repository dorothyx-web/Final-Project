My Favorite Classic Cocktails

A curated collection of 6 timeless cocktail recipes for easy reference when crafting drinks at home.

Whiskey Sour

Rocks glass • Bright, citrusy, and spirit-forward.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 3/4 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2–3/4 oz simple syrup
  • Optional: 1 egg white
  • Ice & lemon/cherry garnish

Instructions

  1. Add whiskey, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to a shaker.
  2. Dry shake for 10–15 seconds (if using egg white).
  3. Add ice and shake again until chilled.
  4. Strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice and garnish.

Highball

Highball glass • Simple, refreshing, and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • 1.5–2 oz whisky
  • 4–6 oz chilled soda water
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon peel for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Pour whisky over the ice.
  3. Top with cold soda water.
  4. Stir gently once or twice and garnish.

Gimlet

Coupe or martini glass • Crisp and lime-forward.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz gin
  • 3/4 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1/2–3/4 oz simple syrup
  • Lime wheel for garnish

Instructions

  1. Add gin, lime juice, and simple syrup to a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake vigorously for 10–15 seconds.
  3. Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.
  4. Garnish with a lime wheel.

Ramos Gin Fizz

Collins glass • Creamy, citrusy, classic New Orleans drink.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz gin
  • 1/2 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • 1 oz heavy cream
  • 1 egg white
  • 2–3 drops orange blossom water
  • Soda water

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients except soda to a shaker.
  2. Dry shake for at least 20 seconds.
  3. Add ice and shake again until very cold.
  4. Strain into a chilled Collins glass and top with soda water.

Piña Colada

Hurricane glass • Tropical and creamy.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1.5 oz cream of coconut
  • 1.5 oz heavy cream
  • 3 oz pineapple juice
  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • Pineapple wedge & cherry

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients with ice until smooth.
  2. Pour into a chilled hurricane glass.
  3. Garnish with pineapple and cherry.

Paloma

Highball glass • Refreshing grapefruit and tequila.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz tequila (blanco or reposado)
  • 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
  • 4 oz grapefruit soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • Grapefruit wedge for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rim a highball glass with salt (optional) and fill with ice.
  2. Add tequila and lime juice.
  3. Top with grapefruit soda and gently stir.
  4. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge.
